Who We Are

LifeStream is a non-profit organization dedicated to saving lives through blood donation. Founded in 2005, we have been connecting blood donors with patients in need for over 15 years.

Our mission is to ensure safe and adequate blood supply to meet the needs of all patients in our community. We work with hospitals, medical centers, and emergency services to provide blood and blood products whenever they are needed.

With your help, we can continue to save lives and make a difference in our community.

Our Services

We provide a range of blood services to the community

💉

Blood Donation

Donate blood at our center or during our mobile blood drives. Your donation can save up to three lives.

🩸

Blood Supply

We supply blood to hospitals and medical centers for surgeries, treatments, and emergencies.

🔍

Blood Testing

All donated blood undergoes rigorous testing to ensure safety and compatibility.

🚑

Emergency Services

We provide emergency blood supply during natural disasters and other emergencies.
